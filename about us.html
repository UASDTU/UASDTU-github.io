The Team

UAS DTU is a team of undergraduate students from Delhi Technological University ( Formerly Delhi College of Engineering) devoted to developing indigenous technological solutions for UAVs.

The team's goal is to develop and harness new technologies in aerospace and defense sector and aid the country in Intelligence, Surveillance, Target Acquisition, and Reconnaissance (ISTAR) and Humanitarian Assistance and Disaster Relief (HADR) . Our ultimate goal is to develop indigenous solutions to reduce India's reliance on COTS products
