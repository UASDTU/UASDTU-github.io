Aarush X1


Aarush is a prototype of the UAV developed with financial resources and engineering mentoring support from Lockheed Martin Corporation. Traffic Management, Geomatics, Mining Surveillance, Border patrol are just some of the areas in which this UAV can be put to effective, efficient use. Aarush X1 Flight Video

Aarush X2


Aarush X2 is the optimized version of Aarush X1. Drawing upon the same design philosophy to retain the precise and dependable flight experience, while featuring a smaller footprint and with reduced fuel consumption, this UAV is meant for missions where vehicle size matters. Aarush X2 Flight Video

Aarush M


Aarush-M is the younger sibling of Aarush. Drawing upon the same design philosophy to retain the precise and dependable flight experience, while featuring a smaller footprint and with reduced fuel consumption, this UAV is meant for missions where vehicle size matters


Tarot


The project aims to explore the hyper spectral remote sensing applications in characterizing plant and soils for quantitative assessment. Plausible results have been obtained by flight testing of a fixed wing UAV and a multi-rotor boarded with infrared and visible wavelength ranged sensors.

Vihaan


Vihaan is a UAS which was developed for primary purpose for competing in SUAS competition in the year 2014. It incorporates a COTS airframe, Skywalker X-8 and uses a dedicated catapult launch system designed and developed in house for short take offs.

Arceus


Arceus offers extremely portable, foldable structure. It is lightweight and suitable for close range undetected surveillance. The payload flaction is 0.8 for the system.


Avniel


Avniel aims to explore the hyper spectral remote sensing applications in characterizing plant and soils for quantitative assessment. Plausible results have been obtained by flight testing of a fixed wing UAV and a multi-rotor boarded with infrared and visible wavelength ranged sensors.

Garuda


GARUDA, a modified Sig Rascal 110 R/C aircraft along with its Ground Control System is capable of performing autonomous flight & navigation, simultaneously gathering actionable surveillance data using optical sensors.

Lazarus


Lazarus, designed to meet the objectives of AUVSI student UAS competition. Lazarus is a modified Sig Rascal 110 R/C aircraft controlled by the ArduPilot Mega, an open-source autopilot.
